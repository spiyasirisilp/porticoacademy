A LITTLE ABOUT OUR AGENCY

Portico Academy was founded when a small group of international and U.S. college admissions experts came togetherwith one goal in mind: to remove the mystique from the college admissions process. It’s simple: we’ve been college admissions officers, high school guidance counselors, and Ivy League international students - so we know how it all works and why. We believe that knowing the “why” goes a long way with understanding the“how” – how to complete applications, how to build your resume, how to write your essays, how to select your colleges, how to seek letters of recommendations, and how to do it all on time. With a combined 30+ year of experience, our accomplished international team has held positions in Ivy League college admissions, U.S. high school guidance, and private college consulting for both international and domestic students.

Portico Academy strives to provide an educational experience through our college admissions support. We provide expert, ethical guidance by way of highly individualized 1:1 support as well as topical workshops. From college planning for 9th -11thgraders, to application and essay support for rising seniors and seniors, Portico Academy counselors inject context, knowledge, and logic into every session, every task, and every bit of guidance. Again, when you know the “why”, the “how” becomes a whole lot easier. And our goal is not just to help you get into the school of your dreams, but to cultivate your development and arm you with knowledge and skills that will help you build the life of your dreams.

EVERY STEP OF THE WAY

College admissions can be a daunting process, especially for international students.

Portico will be with you every step of the way.



THE PORTICO PROMISE


Each student is assigned two personalized college consultants to guide, direct and provide insights to the student. Your student’s dedicated counselor will provide support in areas such as high school planning, summer activities, project management, strategic advising, college fit, and essay editing.

The student takes full ownership of this process, is fully invested and involved in all decisions, and produces all of her own work under the mentorship of a team of dedicated experts.

YOU FIRST, ALWAYS.

Our students come first. That means they get all the attention they need to have a successful admissions process.

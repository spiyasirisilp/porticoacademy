A LITTLE ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Portico Academy was founded in the D.C Metro Area when a small group of international and U.S. college admissions experts came together with one goal in mind: to empower international students through college admissions education. We've been Ivy League college admissions officers, high school guidance counselors, top internal student graduates, and private college admissions consultants - so we know how it all works and why.

We believe that knowing the "why" goes a long way with understanding the "how" - how to complete applications, how to build your resume, how to select best-fit colleges, how to tell your story - and how to do it all on time! With a combined 30+ years of experience, our accomplished bilingual team expertly and ethically arms you with the tools you need to navigate college admissions with authenticity and confidence.
